Our values

Empowering others

The success of the user is our top focus. We succeed if our users do.

Every day, strive to improve

You continue to hold yourself and those you collaborate with to a high standard. Every day you become better.

Become a decent person.

Always go the long way. Do what is right. Put long-term gains ahead of short-term ones.

Make complicated things simple

Complexity is inferior to simplicity. Whatever you set out to do, strive to be the best. An excellent job is its own reward.

Make a crazily significant influence

You set extraordinarily ambitious goals and achieve them. Based on what will generate the greatest value for the user and the business, you set priorities.

Growth

Our intention is for everything we produce and grow to live up to the principles of our Core Values, from the snacks we choose to our company culture and relationships. We do this because we are aware that what we do every day affects how well we are known, how successful we are, and how positively we can affect the health and wellness of others.
